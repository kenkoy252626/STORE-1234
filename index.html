About me

Learn More About Us

Romantic Baboy opened its doors to the public last January 19, 2018, with its first branch at Tomas Morato.

Baboy has a modern Korean ambiance. It is an interior that feels inviting.

Banchan(side dishes). - Kimchi, , Odeng(Fish Cakes), Jap Chae (Korean glass noodle stir fry), Pamuchim, Ssammu(Pickled Radish Paper for Ssam). You can always ask for more. They’re free.

Romantic Baboy offers many types of pork and beef with their special herbs and spices. Made specially for Filipino palate, each type of pork and beef hits all the right things
